Counterbalance Forklift Certification

  • Overview: This program is designed for the most commonly used forklift type, the counterbalance forklift, which is versatile across many industries, from warehousing to manufacturing.
  • Training Focus: Operators learn foundational skills such as load balancing, lifting and lowering techniques, navigating tight spaces, and safely transporting materials..
  • Outcome: Participants gain a certification that validates their skills to operate counterbalance forklifts safely and efficiently in a variety of settings.

Reach Truck Certification

  • Overview: The reach truck certification is tailored for operators working in warehouse environments where narrow aisle storage is prevalent.
  • Training Focus: This program emphasizes skills necessary to safely operate reach trucks, including handling loads at heights, proper stacking and retrieval, and aisle maneuvering..
  • Outcome: Certification that qualifies operators to safely manage reach trucks in tight spaces, high racks, and fast-paced warehouse environments.

Rough Terrain Forklift Certification

  • Overview: This certification is ideal for operators working outdoors on uneven or challenging terrain, such as construction or agricultural sites.
  • Training Focus: This course covers specialized handling techniques for rough terrain forklifts, emphasizing stability, traction, and navigation on sloped or irregular surfaces..
  • Outcome: Operators gain certification to safely handle rough terrain forklifts in outdoor, high-risk environments, reducing the likelihood of accidents and improving site safety.

Rough Terrain Forklift Certification

  • Overview: Specifically for order picker forklifts used in high-bay and high-density storage facilities, this program is essential for operators responsible for picking items from elevated heights.
  • Training Focus: The course provides in-depth training on working at heights, load balancing, order picking techniques, and essential safety practices..
  • Outcome: Certification that qualifies operators to safely manage order picker forklifts, making them valuable assets in warehouses with high-bay storage solutions.
